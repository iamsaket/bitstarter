Search places from anywhere in the world

Browse through are exhaustive listing , that will help tenants find the perfect place for rent. With 10ant, you get all the information of various places at your finger tip. You can even check the photo gallery for better selection.

No more fight for certified owner listings

Credibility is most important at 10ant, hence we have separated the listings from landlord or owner of the place and the listings from broker or third party dealer. We do not want you to suffer and made sure that all the listings are properly screened from the certified owners of the place. In order to achieve this we do a two step verification of the listing before it gets displayed on 10ant. That should give you a peace of mind.

Book places online

Now you don't need to waste your precious time by visiting different places on foot. Book places directly through 10ant service sitting at your home without any hassles. We don't charge for any kind of booking made at 10ant.

Transparent communication

To make transparency in our system we have built in communication tools which will allow the owner & tenant to interact directly. Tenant can book places only if he is satisfied with the owner's response.

Social reviews & feedback

Here at 10ant we take feedback & reviews of our customer seriously. You can leave feedback during or after your stay. We have linked the feedback system with facebook accounts so that your friends can see the reviews & get benefited from the same during there search. After all people believe in word of mouth communication.

10ant wishlist features allow you to get the place of your choice even if it is occupied. Get automatic notifications once the room is available back on our listing. What's more is that you can share your wishlist with your facebook friends. Awesome, isn't it ?

10ant is easily customizable to your needs whether you are a renter or an owner. We tried to incorporate all the unique & interesting features at 10ant which you have always missed in other websites. At 10ant we believe in the mantra of improvisation .

Why Bitcoins?
Bitcoins are the future of money on the Internet. No fees, no exchanges, just simple person to person exchange. Bitcoins allow you to connect with people across the world without having to deal with the hassle of regular currencies. Watch this video of Bitcoin on Youtube.
How to pay & support funding?
Pay using your credit card or digital wallet by clicking on the 'Preorder button'. Help Bitstart our site by sending us bitcoins, backers will recieve the ability to post promoted posts on the site and get their names listed on our contribution page :) .
How is 10ant different from other real estate websites and classified ads?
10ant has been made from ground up to include those shortcomings that you often find in other websites. It doesn't allow third party dealers to list places for rent.This makes search more easier. You can also know what the actual place is like by reading thier reviews or checking the photo gallery.
